You may be asking... What exactly is Valorant?

Valorant is a 5v5 tactical shooter game developed and published by RIOT games for Microsoft Windows. This game features a plethora of characters in which precise gunplay meets unique agent abilities. To learn more about this game please visit the official Valorant website.

To give an idea of what each character does

The first character on the left, is named "Phoenix". He is a character who tries to take one-on-one duels to allow his team to push onto sites. His abilities allow him to flash certain areas on the map in order to clear it out from enemies, throw a molotov to halt any pushes, a fire wall to block vision and lastly an ultimate ability that gives him a second chance!

The second character, is named "Reyna". She is a character who works similar to Phoenix in the sense that she makes space for her team to push onto enemy sites. Her abilities are a bit different but still allow her to flash certain areas on the map in order to clear it out from enemies, turn invisible for a few seconds, suck on slain souls to gain more health and lastly, an ultimate that gives her fast shooting speeds and the ability to go invincible again!

Our last character, is named "Killjoy". She is a character who works a lot different compared to the other two. Killjoy is a defensive expert who can lock down areas on the map and watch flanks. Her abilities allow her to set alarm bots that trigger upon enemy movement, activatable grenades, a turret that fires automatically at enemies and finally, her ultimate which locksdown a portion of the map detaining any enemies within it.
